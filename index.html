§ 01 / WHAT'S INSIDE

WHAT'S INSIDE.

We didn't build a feature list. We built the things you actually use between sets — and stopped there.

01
Three Routines.
Switch Tabs.
Legs. Chest + Back. Arms. Each one is its own page — open the app on leg day and there's nothing else in the way. Tap the tab, lift, close it.
02
Supersets,
Linked.
Two exercises become a superset with one tap. A curly brace runs down the side — exactly the way your trainer scribbles it in the corner of your program.
03
Weights
Carry Over.
Today's weight is where you start next time. No re-typing every week. Bump it up half a kilo, knock it down — the point is to lift, not to log.
04
Monthly
Mirror.
Mark exercises done and the session quietly logs itself. End of the month, see which days you actually showed up and where the numbers moved. No streaks. No guilt.
§ 02 / TRY IT

HAVE A POKE.

The real app, embedded right here. Tap a session. Bump a weight. Mark a set done. Long-press an exercise to drop yourself a note.

Nothing saves on this page — when you've decided you like it, install it on your phone and your data lives there.
